What is DraftStudios?

Draft Studios is a User Experience/User Interface (UX/UI) design and web development agency powered by Coded by Kids students. In Draft Studios, student teams (ages 15-24) work on projects for small to mid-sized nonprofits and businesses that help them hone the professional and technical skills they’ve learned in CBK programs. As intermediate and advanced level students, Draft Studios team members learn concepts that include user research, front and back-end web development, requirements gathering, and agile project management. During projects, all students are supported by tech and startup professionals that act as mentors and advisors.
